Collexion

Collexion is a hackerspace, a technology and artistic collaborative space, in Lexington, KY.

We are located at 109 E. Loudon Ave. Lexington, KY 40505. Regular meetings are Wednesdays at 6pm. See our open hours for the best time to come by.

Join our email list. Party down in our IRC chatroom.

Participate in Collexion's Current Events and subscribe to our official Calendar of Events.

Give Back to Collexion: Donate Online (the BGCF handles all our funds, be sure to select "Collexion Fund")

Join in on our weekly IRC meetup: Wednesdays 9pm on irc.freenode.net in #collexion
